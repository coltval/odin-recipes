Colton's Special Scramp or Chicken

What you will need to get from the store

  • Chicken or Shrimp
  • Red Onion
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cilantro
  • Fennagreek
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Fun

    • First, marinate chiken or shrimp in a mixture of 2 eggs, fennugreek, salt, pepper, cilantro, lime

    Fry marinated protein in oil cooking on both sides, remove from oil and step aside

    add tomatoes, onions, peppers to oil, cook until soft

    add red chilli powder, fennugreek and other spices

    add coconut oil and cook down until thick

    add cooked protein back to the pot

    serve!
