Air Fried Chicken!

  • 2lb of chicken thighs
  • Air Fryer

Instructions

  1. Take your chicken and place them in your aif fryer
  2. Make sure that it's clean
  3. Spray oil on top of chicken, spread seasoning evenly
  4. Air fry for 9-10 minutes at 380 degrees
  5. Flip and repeat step 3-4
  6. rest for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
